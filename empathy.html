INTRODUCTION

The way Cleveland State University Students managing their Academics & Job.

This study conducted through design thinking approaches, Understanding the daily experiences of Cleveland State University students who try to balance Work & Academics. The goal is to Identify their challenges, struggles, faced as an International Student, who has come with many dreams, but as their path is not easy as their dreams.

This study tries to cover the real cost of life, as this study does not cover any emotional struggle, as they leave their lovely mother who always cares a lot and father who sustains the wants and needs of them.

It covers only half a story on how they manage their time between academics and duty they must perform to survive, how this dual commitment impacts them mentally. I went to empathize with students to define the truth of their problems and ideated a few solutions to try to develop upon their experience.

This [Problem Statement] , Students, particularly those who balance work and studies, face a significant challenge in managing their time, academic workload and finances which is leading the way to path of stress and frustration. They need an effective tool to help them organize their schedules, track academic progress, manage finances, and a tool to reduce anxiety and mental stress to achieve balance and wellbeing because life goes through it however we are. It’s a lie if no stress is involved in this journey.

Define

Point of View

I need a way to efficiently manage my time, stay on top of my academics and keep my finances in order while also having a tool to help me with the stress and anxiety that come with these challenges, so I can maintain my wellbeing.

User

Master’s student in computer science who works part time in parking services and as a research assistant.

Needs

Time management (studying during breaks), academic organization (much better than blackboard), financial control to manage money carefully, and stress relief.

Goal

Succeed academically and manage wellbeing despite the pressures of a job for financial support and sustenance.

How Might We

How might we design integrated tools that can help students efficiently manage their time, academic tasks, and finances, and also support mental stress to enhance their academic success and wellbeing?

IDEATE & PROTOTYPE

Solutions for Student Success and Wellbeing

1

Integrated Tool of Education

Enhance the existing course work solution like Blackboard by integrating with time management tool to study in less time and can easily tap in study mode after checking the course work without defocusing or distraction and can be a centralized hub where students can access learning content, track their progress and finances, and connect with CSU supports seamlessly, which was not available in existing solution with Blackboard.

Scientific Technique as a Tool for Stress Relief

Mental stress or anxiety reduction tool to address the emotional side and mental side effects which are faced by working students. This solution offers a Breathwork session, which is so free, use it anywhere, any time and at any location to relieve stress from work.
